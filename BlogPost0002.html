Arts of the website.

After reading many posts from websites such as Wait But Why and watching youtube channels such as CGP Grey, I feel inspired to add stickman art to the website to elaborate on what I am talking about. The web art will not be as detailed as theirs but I hope it adds value to the website as a whole. I will also need to decide on a colour for the website. As the colours are implemented in the CSS, users of the website can easily change the colours of the text and background if they find the default stylesheet too obnoxious.
